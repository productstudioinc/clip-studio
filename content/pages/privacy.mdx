Thank you for choosing **clip.studio**. At **clip.studio**, the privacy and security of our users are of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect and how we use this information.

#### 1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Information: When you use **clip.studio**, you may provide information such as email, name, and billing details for subscription and support purposes.

1.2 Automatic Data Collection: Our service collects data from user interactions to improve responsiveness and accuracy. This includes questions asked and the responses provided.

1.3 Cookies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information.

#### 2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision: We use the information we collect primarily to provide, maintain, and improve our services.

2.2 Communication: To notify you about changes to our services, provide support, or send administrative messages.

2.3 Enhancement: To understand user behavior and improve our services.

2.4 Google and YouTube API Services: Our service uses YouTube API Services. By using our service, you are agreeing to be bound by the [YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms). Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements. We use Google services for login authentication and to connect to YouTube for uploading media generated from our site. For more information on how Google handles your data, please refer to the [Google Privacy Policy](http://www.google.com/policies/privacy).

2.5 Sharing: We do not share your information with third-party AI platforms.

#### 3. Data Storage & Protection

All collected data is stored on secure servers with the latest encryption technology. We strive to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

#### 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share anonymized data with trusted partners for research and analysis purposes.

#### 5. User Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal data. You can also request what personal data we hold about you and the purpose of its storage. To revoke clip.studio's access to your Google/YouTube data, you can visit the [Google security settings page](https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions) and remove access for our application.

#### 6. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your stored data related to YouTube API Services, please contact our support team at support@clip.studio. We will process your request and delete the relevant data within a reasonable timeframe.

#### 7. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help improve user experience by remembering user preferences. If you prefer, you can set your browser to refuse all cookies.

#### 8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

#### 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes and encourage everyone to review this policy periodically.

#### 10. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request information about your stored data, please contact our support team at support@clip.studio.
