### **How It Works**

1. **Write a Theme**: Write a theme for your video and have the AI generate a script for you.
2. **Generate Images**: Generate images based on the provided descriptions from AI, or modify them yourself and regenerate if you want.
3. **Review and Edit Script**: Read the script, make any changes if needed, then generate the voiceover.
4. **Render and Export**: Render the video and export it directly to social media platforms.
